Pasta Bake!

Family fun for all!

In the beginning, there was only pasta. Then God cried out "LET THERE BE BAKE!"

Ingredients

  • 500g mince
  • 5 handfuls of pasta, 6 for Stephanie
  • CHEESE
  • pasta bake sauce

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Add pasta to pot
  3. After a few minutes start cooking mince in pan
  4. When Pasta is a bit soft and mince is cooked add to baking tray
  5. Mix both with pasta sauce till it's spread around
  6. Add CHEESE to the dish
  7. Cook in oven at 180 degrees untill golden brown

    8. GORGE
