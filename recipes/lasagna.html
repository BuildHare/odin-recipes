Lasagna!

A tasty treat for you to eat!

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food = homemade lasagna. Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 13 ounces fresh lasagna sheets
  • 17 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
  • White Sauce
  • Meat Sauce

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large pot, add veg.
  2. Add beef and pork, break it up with spoon in pot till browned.
  3. Cook with love.

Enjoy!
